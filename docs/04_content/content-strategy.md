# Content Strategy & Tone of Voice

*Owner: Content Creator / Copy Strategist*

## 1. The Core Voice: "Quietly Confident"
Our voice must be the antithesis of the anxiety-inducing furniture purchase. We do not yell. We do not use loud promotional language. We do not beg for trust; we earn it through exactness, clarity, and calm authority.

*   **Intelligent & Warm:** We write for a cosmopolitan Nigerian audience that appreciates elevated living.
*   **Exact & Deliberate:** No vague adjectives. "High quality" is banned. "Kiln-dried Nigerian Mahogany" is required.
*   **Concierge Tone:** We speak like a knowledgeable, unhurried interior design consultant guiding a high-net-worth client.

### Words to Avoid vs. Words to Use
*   *Avoid:* Cheap, Best Quality, Hurry, Buy Now, Luxuriate, Affordable, Trending.
*   *Use:* Crafted, Exact, Reliable, Crated, Kiln-dried, Deliberate, Anchor, Scale, Dispatch, Acquire.

## 3. Product Page (PDP) Copy Architecture

The PDP is our primary "Reassurance Engine." The content must flow logically to dismantle purchase anxiety.

**1. The Editorial Introduction (The "Why")**
A vivid, atmospheric description of how the piece anchors a room.
*   *Example:* "The Noka Sofa does not shout. Its deep, architectural lines and structured low back offer a grounded, contemporary presence, making it the quiet centerpiece of a well-edited living space."

**2. The Material Breakdown (The "How It's Made")**
Hard facts over adjectives.
*    **Frame:** Kiln-dried, locally sourced Mansonia wood.
*    **Suspension:** High-tensile sinuous springs for sustained structural lift.
*    **Cushioning:** Multi-layered, high-resilience 35kg density foam.
*    **Upholstery:** Heavyweight tactile weave (45,000 double rubs).

**3. The Dimensions (The "Fit")**
Always accompanied by the visual diagram.
*   "Overall Width: 220cm | Depth: 95cm | Height: 75cm"

**4. The Delivery Promise (The "When & How")**
Dynamic, exact language.
*   *Example:* "Dispatches in 14-21 days. Includes premier White-Glove installation in your room of choice if located in Lagos or Abuja."

## 4. Checkout & Microcopy (The "Contractual" Clarity)

The checkout tone shifts from editorial to pure, reassuring operational exactness. We are confirming a contract.

*   **Cart Drawer Empty:** "Your space is a blank canvas. Find the pieces that anchor it."
*   **Checkout Header:** "Secure Checkout. Your order details are confirmed on the right."
*   **Shipping Selector:** 
    *   *Option 1:* "White-Glove Installation (Lagos/Abuja) - We bring it inside, assemble it, and remove all packaging."
    *   *Option 2:* "Standard Crated Delivery (Nationwide) - Delivered securely crated to your door."
*   **Payment Button:** "Place Secure Order" (Instead of just "Pay").

## 5. Post-Purchase Communications (The "Purgatory Fix")
These emails or SMS messages must sound like a dedicated concierge, not a robot.

*   **Order Confirmed:** "Your vision is secured. We have received your order for the [Item Name], and production scheduling begins tomorrow."
*   **Assembly Started:** "Update from the floor: Our artisans have begun the final upholstery on your piece. It is taking shape beautifully."
*   **Ready for Dispatch:** "Your piece has passed our final structural checks and is handed to our White-Glove team. They will contact you shortly to schedule your installation window."
