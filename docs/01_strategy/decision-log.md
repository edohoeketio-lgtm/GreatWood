# Phase 1 Strategy Decision Log

## Decisions Made (2026-03-10)

1.  **Core Positioning:** Adopted "We are selling confidence..." as the central brand tenet. This shifts the focus from product-first to trust-first marketing, fundamentally altering how UX and copy will be approached.
2.  **Competitive Stance:** Explicitly decided *not* to compete with Taeillo on volume, bright colors, or overarching "playful" African identities. We are claiming the "quiet luxury / editorial" space.
3.  **The 5-Shot Minimum Rule:** Instituted a rigid requirement for product photography to combat "what I ordered vs. what I got" anxiety. This dictates UX requirements for the PDP.
4.  **Checkout Philosophy:** Decided against standard "frictionless" 1-click checkouts in favor of a "deliberate, contract-like" checkout experience to reinforce the premium, high-ticket nature of the transaction.
5.  **Post-Purchase Strategy:** Identified the waiting period as a critical trust-building phase ("Post-Purchase Purgatory Fix"). This will require specific UX flows and CRM integrations later in the build.
